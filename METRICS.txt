mechanism:  QEMU user-mode TCG translates each BB on first encounter and
            inserts an instrumentation callback at every BB boundary; the
            callback hashes (prev_loc, cur_loc) into the 64 K edge map.
            Compile-time afl-clang-fast knows the CFG statically and inlines
            counter increments at LLVM-IR branch points, so its edges
            correspond to source-level decisions while QEMU's are
            BB-transition-level.
